From AP Calculus to AP Business
How McLean's course offerings have evolved
Core AP Foundation
1990s–2000
McLean's program centered on AP U.S. History, English Literature, Biology, and Calculus. All advanced learning was strictly academic and lecture-based.
Technology Advances
2000s–2010s
McLean expanded to computer-based electives and began integrating technology into existing courses. Students started using digital tools for research.
Covid Remote Learning
2020–2022
McLean shifted fully online. Zoom classes and digital assignments became standard across all courses.
Hybrid Learning
2022–Present
In-person learning returns, but digital platforms remain essential to daily instruction.
AP Business Principles
2026–Future
McLean introduces AP Business Principles, focused on:
  • Finance
  • Marketing
  • Entrepreneurship
  • Business Strategy
